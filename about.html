Meet Soraya

Spiritual Kinesiologist & Vibration Artist

My Journey to Healing

For years, I carried emotions I couldn't name — a heaviness that showed up in my body, my relationships, and my creative blocks. Traditional approaches helped, but something was missing. When I discovered Kinesiology and the concept of trapped emotions, everything changed.

I trained as a Spiritual Kinesiologist and found my calling: helping others release what no longer serves them. Along the way, I discovered that art became a powerful companion to this work — a way to express the inexpressible and anchor new frequencies into being.

Why I Created SoulArt Temple

I believe healing should be accessible, gentle, and deeply personal. SoulArt Temple was born from my desire to share these transformative tools with anyone seeking emotional freedom. Whether you're processing grief, releasing inherited patterns, or simply wanting to feel more like yourself — you belong here.

My work is rooted in compassion, intention, and transformation. I hold space for your journey without judgment, knowing that your inner wisdom is your greatest guide.

My Approach

Every session and tool I create is infused with the understanding that you already hold the answers within you. My role is simply to help you access them. Through muscle testing, affirmations, and creative expression, we work together to identify and release the emotional blocks that keep you from living fully.

I combine ancient wisdom with practical techniques, creating a bridge between the spiritual and the everyday. Whether you're working with me directly or using the digital tools here, you'll find the same gentle, trauma-aware approach that honors your unique journey.

With love and blessings,
Soraya

FAQ for Spiritual Newcomers

What are Stored Shadow Emotions?

These are emotions that have become stuck in our energy field — from our own experiences, inherited from ancestors, or absorbed from others. They can influence our thoughts, behaviours, and physical wellbeing without us realising.

What is muscle testing (Kinesiology)?

Muscle testing is a gentle technique that uses the body's subtle responses to identify what is true for you at a subconscious level. It helps us communicate with the body's wisdom to find stored emotions and beliefs.

Do I need any experience to use SoulArt Temple?

Not at all. Our Discovery Portal and Education Centre will guide you through everything you need to know. The tools are designed to be gentle, intuitive, and accessible to everyone.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. SoulArt Temple is a complementary tool for personal exploration and spiritual growth. If you are experiencing mental health challenges, please seek support from a qualified professional.

How often should I use the tools?

Listen to your body. Some people use the Quick Release daily, others weekly. There is no right frequency — only what feels aligned for you.
