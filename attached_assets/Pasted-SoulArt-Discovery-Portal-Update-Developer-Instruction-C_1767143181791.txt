SoulArt Discovery Portal Update – Developer Instruction & Content Outline

⸻

Purpose: To create an interactive, educational, and spiritual initiation page for members beginning their journey into identifying and understanding Stored Shadow Emotions. This Discovery Portal will serve as a foundational experience before using the Quick Release tool or upgrading to deeper decoding.

⸻

1. Welcome & Sacred Entry Prompt

Content Block:

“Welcome. This is your time. Take a deep breath, ground yourself, and prepare to meet what has been waiting to be seen.”

	•	Display this as an animated text fade-in on page load.

2. Optional Spiritual Blessing Prompt

Instructional Prompt:

“Before beginning, you may wish to offer a blessing or energetic seal to your space. This creates a protected and intentional field for your journey.”

Sample Blessing:

“I invite only truth, light, grace, and clarity into this space. May this session reveal what is ready to be seen and released.”

	•	Include a button: “Add Your Own Blessing” (textarea opens for user entry – stored locally).

⸻

3. Shadow Emotion Category Grid

Interactive Grid Categories:
	•	Common
	•	Ancestral
	•	Heartwall
	•	Etheric
	•	Aura/Field
	•	DNA
	•	Third Trimester
	•	Twin (Dual)
	•	Deeply Hidden
	•	Stealth
	•	Body Coded (map)

Functionality:
	•	When user clicks a category, show the following prompt:

“This is a [Category] stored shadow emotion.”

	•	Allow user to muscle test or inner-check (no response needed by app).
	•	Follow-up prompt:

“Do I have more than 50 stored shadow emotions in this category?”
“Do I have more than 100… less than 150…?”

Input Field:
	•	Allow user to manually enter a number (optional feature, stored locally or to premium tracker).
	•	Add button: “Add to My Tracker (Premium Only)” – disabled unless user is logged in with access.

⸻

4. Layering Awareness (Nested Emotions)

Instructional Text:

“Some shadow emotions may be layered – hiding beneath one another. You may identify a surface emotion, then discover another beneath it. Simply ask: ‘Is this emotion layered?’ and proceed as guided.”

	•	Toggle or checkbox option: “This category contains layered emotions” (optional local storage).

⸻

5. Summary Screen (After User Identifies Categories)

Display:
	•	List of categories with numbers (if entered) and any notes.
	•	Button: “Save to Journal” or “Download My Discovery Summary”

Prompt:

“Would you like to begin your Quick Release now or continue learning in the Education Centre?”

	•	“Yes, begin Quick Release” → Directs to Quick Release Module
	•	“Explore More” → Directs to Education Centre

⸻

6. Membership Upgrade Logic

Display Message (after first Discovery Session):

“You’ve completed your Discovery Session. You can now begin three free self-sessions using the Quick Release tool. For deeper decoding categories and tracking, upgrade to SoulArt Temple Premium.”
