Privacy Notice

At SoulArt Temple, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (email address, name)
  • Journal entries and personal reflections you choose to save
  • Discovery session data and emotion tracking
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your membership and payments
  • Save your progress and personal journey data
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about your account

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal journal entries or emotional data with third parties.

Your Sacred Space

Your healing journey is private. The content you create within SoulArt Temple, including journal entries, discovery sessions, and artwork, belongs to you. We do not access, share, or analyze your personal content for any purpose other than providing our services to you.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. These are necessary for the proper functioning of our services.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Notice or our data practices, please contact us through our website.
