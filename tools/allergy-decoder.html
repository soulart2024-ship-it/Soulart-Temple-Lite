Burn Paper Protocol

A sacred ritual for releasing what no longer serves you:

  1. Write down on paper what you are releasing - the sensitivity, the emotion, the belief
  2. Hold the paper and speak aloud: "I release this from my energy field with love and gratitude for the lessons learned"
  3. Safely burn the paper in a fireproof container (a candle flame, fireplace, or outdoor fire pit)
  4. As the smoke rises, visualize the energy transforming and returning to source
  5. When the paper is fully burned, say: "It is done. I am free."
  6. Dispose of the ashes outside, returning them to the earth

Safety note: Always burn paper in a safe, well-ventilated area with water nearby.

Joyful Movement Practices

Choose a practice that resonates with your body right now:

Shake It Off (2-3 minutes)

Stand with feet hip-width apart. Begin shaking your hands, then arms, shoulders, and let the shaking move through your whole body. Shake out the old energy with enthusiasm!

Victory Dance (1-2 minutes)

Put on your favorite uplifting music and dance freely. Celebrate your release! Move however feels good - no judgment, just joy.

Heart Opening Stretch

Stand tall, interlace fingers behind your back, lift your chest and gently stretch your arms back. Breathe deeply into your heart space. Hold for 5 breaths.

Grounding Stomp (1 minute)

March in place with intention, stomping your feet firmly. With each stomp, imagine releasing the old energy down through your feet into the earth for transformation.

Spiral Arms

Extend arms out to sides. Slowly spiral them upward, gathering energy, then spiral down, releasing. Repeat 3-5 times, breathing deeply.
